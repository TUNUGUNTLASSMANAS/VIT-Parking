About IET

The Institution of Engineering and Technology is a multidisciplinary professional engineering institution.

  • Represents the engineering profession in matters of public concern.
  • Assists governments to make the public aware of engineering and technological issues.
  • Has an educational role, seeking to support its members through their careers by offering a professional home for life, to secure the future of engineering.

About IET-VIT

IET On Campus VIT is the student representation group of Europe's largest professional society.

  • Committed to sharing and spreading STEM knowledge among the student community.
  • Help students become standouts by providing them training and inspiration unavailable in the confines of their classrooms.
  • Regularly organize university and global level events like workshops, entrepreneurship events and hackathons.
